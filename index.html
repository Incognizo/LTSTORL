Stay On The Right Lane!

Here, underclassmen are able to find information to help their transition into highschool.




Stay On The Right Lane is a website created by Andrew and Katharine Bonilla. (mostly andrew though)

- Ok but why? -

The transition into highschool is not easy, especially at Lane with its huge student body and expansive building. The purpose of this website is to compile and share information that underclassmen may find useful. For example, what courses should I take? What are courses? How should I write an email to my teacher? That sort of thing.
